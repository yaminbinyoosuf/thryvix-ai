Data Deletion

Your right to delete your data · DPDP Act 2023 compliant

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from THRYVIX AI systems. We take this right seriously and will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

How to Request Data Deletion

01

Send a deletion request

Email us at privacy@thryvixai.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request" or WhatsApp us at +91 75920 63527

02

Include your details

Provide your name, phone number, and the clinic name where your data was collected. This helps us locate your records.

03

Verification

We will verify your identity before processing the deletion to protect against unauthorized requests.

04

Confirmation

We will confirm deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation message once your data has been permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your personal profile (name, phone number, date of birth)
  • Appointment history and records
  • Medical records and prescriptions
  • WhatsApp conversation history with our bot
  • Health ID and associated wallet data

What Cannot Be Deleted

  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Records required for legal or regulatory compliance
  • Billing records required for accounting purposes (retained 7 years per Indian law)

For Clinic Owners

To delete your entire clinic account and all associated data, please contact us at privacy@thryvixai.com. Account deletion includes all patient records, appointment history, and clinic data. This action is irreversible.

Facebook / Meta Data

If you used our WhatsApp bot via Facebook's platform, you can also request data deletion through Facebook's data deletion tools. Visit facebook.com/help/delete_account for more information.
