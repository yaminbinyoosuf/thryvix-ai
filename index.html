Frequently asked questions.

What technology powers the infrastructure?+

We use Make, n8n, and Zapier for automation, Google Sheets for data architecture, WhatsApp Business API for communication, and Python for custom logic. The tools are secondary — we design platform-agnostic logic so your workflows survive any tool change.

Does my team need technical expertise?+

No. We design for operators, not engineers. You approve the logic in plain language, we handle all the mechanics. Every delivery includes a non-technical walkthrough, operator guide, and video documentation.

What if something breaks?+

Every system includes a failure monitoring layer. You know within minutes if anything stops working. All retainer plans cover ongoing fixes, updates, and continuous monitoring. That is the core purpose of the partnership.

Can you work with our existing tools?+

Yes. Our diagnostic audit maps your current tech stack first. We don't rip and replace what works — we build connective tissue to close operational gaps and unify your existing tools into one coherent system.

How long does deployment take?+

Starter systems: 5–10 business days. Growth Ops: 2–4 weeks. Scale Ops: phased over 6–12 weeks. We never begin building before completing the full diagnostic — rushing that step creates expensive rework.

Do you offer INR pricing?+

Yes. Starter System: ₹41,500 one-time. Growth Ops: ₹49,800/month + ₹2,07,500 setup. Scale Ops: ₹83,000+/month with custom setup. Message us on WhatsApp for a tailored INR quote based on your exact requirements and exchange rate at time of engagement.

What integrations do you support?+

WhatsApp Business API, Google Sheets, Make, n8n, Zapier, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, LinkedIn, TikTok, GA4, HubSpot, Calendly, and most CRMs. Custom integrations via Python for anything beyond this list. Self-hosting available on DigitalOcean or AWS with Docker containers.
