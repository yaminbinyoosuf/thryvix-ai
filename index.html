// The Daily Reality

What running a clinic in Kerala actually looks like

01
Missed Calls = Missed Patients
Clinics receive dozens of calls daily that go unanswered. Each missed call is a patient who called the next clinic instead.
30% of calls go unanswered
02
Staff Burning Out on Admin
Front desk staff spend 4+ hours daily on manual appointment management, confirmation calls, and WhatsApp replies.
4hrs daily admin per staff member
03
No-Shows Kill Revenue
Average no-show rate is 15–22%. For a clinic doing 25 appointments at ₹500 average, that’s 4–5 missed slots every single day.
₹40,000+ lost monthly
04
Running Blind
Most clinics have no idea which hours are busiest, which doctors have the most no-shows, or what their monthly revenue looks like.
Zero data. Zero visibility.
05
Doctors Walk In Blind
Doctor sees a name on a register — no history, no medications, no treatment plan. Every consultation starts cold.
Slower consults. Lower trust.
06
Software That Does Not Fit
Existing HMS tools are expensive, complex, and not built for how Indian patients communicate — on WhatsApp, in Malayalam, at midnight.
Wrong product. Wrong market.
