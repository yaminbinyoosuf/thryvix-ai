Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately

THRYVIX AI ("THRYVIX", "we", "us", or "our") operates thryvixai.com and the THRYVIX Health platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Clinic information: Clinic name, owner name, doctor name, city, email, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Patient information: Name, phone number, date of birth, appointment history, and medical records entered by clinic staff.
  • WhatsApp messages: Appointment notifications and communications sent via WhatsApp after patients book on the clinic's web booking page.
  • Usage data: How you interact with our portal and services.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide clinic management services including appointment booking, reminders, and records.
  • To send automated WhatsApp notifications to patients on behalf of clinics.
  • To generate analytics and reports for clinic owners.
  • To improve our platform and services.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on AWS servers located in Mumbai, India (ap-south-1 region). We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act). Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using industry-standard encryption.

4. Patient Data and Consent

Patient data is collected and processed on behalf of clinics (our customers). Clinics are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from their patients. Patients may request deletion of their data by contacting their clinic directly or emailing us at privacy@thryvixai.com.

5. WhatsApp Communication

We use Meta's WhatsApp Business API to send appointment reminders and notifications. By providing your phone number to a THRYVIX-powered clinic, you consent to receive WhatsApp messages related to your appointments. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with:

  • The clinic whose services you are using
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) for message delivery
  • AWS for secure cloud infrastructure

7. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@thryvixai.com

8. Data Retention

We retain clinic and patient data for as long as the clinic is an active customer plus 12 months. After account termination, data is permanently deleted within 30 days upon request.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18 without parental consent. For minor patients, parental/guardian consent is required.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify active customers of significant changes via email or WhatsApp.
